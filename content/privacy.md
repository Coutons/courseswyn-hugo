As users of the internet, we care about privacy just like you. And as fellow human beings, we think you deserve to know exactly what you can expect from your Comidoc experience. Before you read our privacy policy, we'd first like to tell you how we think about privacy in simple terms.

We do not sell personal information about anyone who uses our services. Ever. That wouldn't be fair.

We created Comidoc because the existing model for online courses shopping doesn't work well for most people. People shop in isolation, spend countless hours searching for quality courses and never know if they're getting the best price.

So we decided to build a community that uses the collective insights of our users and members to power tools that allow everyone to shop smarter, together. Similar to applications that enable commuters to cut through traffic and save time, which are powered by their users and members providing information about speed and congestion, Comidoc users and members share real-time information with the rest of the community and help everyone save time and money in the process.

Want to know if a promo coupon code works? Sure, another member just tested all of the codes with Comidoc five minutes ago. Want to see when a course you're interested in goes on sale? Another Comidoc member is helping us update the price right now. When shoppers hunt for deals together, rather than on their own, everyone saves time and money. Everybody wins.

We understand this community network approach isn't for everyone and that's okay. We think the vast majority of our users and members will see the value of sharing things like successful promo codes and price drops with each other and will learn first-hand how it helps everyone save a lot of time and money. But, it's up to you, and we give you the choice to opt out at any time.

Our stance on privacy is simple: We will be transparent with what data we collect and how we use it to save you time and money, and you can decide if you're good with that.

If this sounds fair, then let's start shopping smarter, together.

## Introducing the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") explains how CoursesWyn collects and processes information from members and users of the website, software applications, and other online services we provide (collectively "CoursesWyn's products"). CoursesWyn's products include, but are not limited to, the CoursesWyn extension for desktop web browsers (the "Extension"), the CoursesWyn mobile application (the "CoursesWyn Mobile App"), and the website located at https://courseswyn.com (the "Website").

The Privacy Policy sets out the basis on which we will process information we collect from you, or that you provide to us, in connection with your use of CoursesWyn's products, regardless of how you access or use them. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully so that you understand your rights in relation to how we use and process information that you share and that we collect.

CoursesWyn ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.
This Privacy Policy explains:

What information we collect and why
How we use cookies and similar technologies
How we share information (including Google AdSense)
Your choices & EU/California rights
How to contact us
## 1. Information We Collect
Account data — e-mail, hashed password, preferred language.
Usage data — pages visited, clicks on coupon-codes, referring URLs.
Device data — browser type, OS, approximate geolocation (city-level, from IP).
Optional community data — promo-code submissions, price alerts you create.
We do not collect payment-card numbers or sell personal information.

## 2. Cookies & Local Storage
We use first-party cookies to:

Keep you signed in
Remember site preferences
Measure aggregated traffic (Plausible, Umami, IP-anonymised)
Third-party cookies (Google AdSense) may be set to deliver personalised or contextual ads. You can disable non-essential cookies at any time via our Cookie Settings link in the footer.

## 3. How We Use & Share Data
We use data to (1) provide and improve CoursesWyn; (2) detect fraud; (3) show relevant content or ads.

Data may be shared with:

Infrastructure vendors (AWS, Cloudflare, Bunny.net)
Analytics (Plausible, Umami)
Advertising (Google AdSense) — ads may be personalised; see Google's Ad Privacy & Terms.
All sharing is governed by GDPR-compliant Data Processing Agreements.

## 4. Your Choices & Rights
You can → update profile data, download a copy, or delete your account from My Account → Settings.

EEA/UK/Swiss users may exercise GDPR rights; California residents may invoke CCPA rights (opt-out of "sale" of personal info — which we do not perform).

E-mail privacy@courseswyn.com for any request; we respond within 30 days.

## 5. Data Security & Retention
HTTPS enforced; passwords hashed with bcrypt.
Back-ups encrypted at rest.
We retain account data while your account is active and for up to 12 months after deletion for audit/legal reasons, then purge it.
